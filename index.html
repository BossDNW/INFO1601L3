Poketry

The Adventure Begins,
if you think you can stand the heat,
jump the gun and light the pyre,
lots of flaming friends to meet,
so step on up and choose fire,

 but if you’d rather sail the sea,
come make your world a little broader,
to join the surfing with unrivaled glee,
make sure your partner is water,

 or perhaps you prefer beautiful flowers,
or like a creature with a bit more sass,
or want other to forget your power,
well then, my friend, you were made for grass,

 amazing adventures out there await,
the story is yours so don’t hesitate.
